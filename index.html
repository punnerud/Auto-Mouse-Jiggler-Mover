Auto Mouse Jiggler Mover

Keep your Mac awake and prevent screen timeouts with this intelligent mouse jiggler

Intelligent Battery Management

Option to pause when on battery power to conserve energy. The app intelligently manages power consumption to maximize battery life.

Customizable Intervals

Choose from preset jiggle intervals or create your own custom timing. Perfect for tailoring the app to your specific needs.

Menu Bar App

Runs quietly in your menu bar, giving you complete control over when and how it operates without cluttering your desktop.

Security Conscious

Option to disable when your screen is locked, preventing unwanted mouse movements during secure moments.

Zero Configuration

Works right out of the box with sensible defaults, while still offering power-user features for those who want more control.

Privacy Focused

Operates entirely on your local machine with no internet connection required and no data collection.

Perfect For

• Remote workers needing to maintain "active" status
• Preventing interruptions during presentations
• Keeping your Mac awake during downloads
• Avoiding timeout issues in remote desktop sessions
• Maintaining connection to virtual meetings
